Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

ChoreUp for Families ("the App") is designed with your family's privacy in mind.

Data Collection

The App stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Information Stored Locally

  • Child profiles (names and avatars)
  • Chores, rewards, and goals you create
  • Points and completion history
  • Photos taken for chore proof (stored only on your device)

Camera Usage

The App requests camera access solely to allow children to take photos as proof of chore completion. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Notifications

The App may send local notifications to remind parents about pending approvals. No data is sent to external services.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed for family use. All data remains on the device under parental control. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by not collecting any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
